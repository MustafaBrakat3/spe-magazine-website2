SPE Cairo University Student Chapter

The Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE) Cairo University Student Chapter is one of the most active student chapters in the Middle East. Founded in 1987, we are dedicated to fostering technical knowledge, professional development, and leadership skills among petroleum engineering students at Cairo University.
